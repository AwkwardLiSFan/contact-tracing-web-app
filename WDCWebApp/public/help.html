FAQs

Below, you can browse the most frequently asked questions on a variety of topics, from using our app to staying safe from COVID-19:

Using the App

Our app is extremely simple to use. New to the app? Sign up using the 'Register' link on the top-right of the page. Already a registered user? Simply click on 'Login' next to Register in order to access your user dashboard. Once logged in, you can record your visits, check the logs, access a map to observe current active hotspots and edit your profile!

From your user dashboard, click on the check-in button. In the pop-up window, simply enter the venue code and press submit! The app will let you know if your check-in was successful or if the code was invalid. In case of any other technical difficulties, please contact the manager of the establishment for further assistance.

Please make sure you login as an admin, you'll automatically get access to your admin dashboard after doing that. From here, you can sign up new health officials, update hotspots, examine check-in history for any user or venue and much more.

Yes! You can sign up as a venue owner. Our customized dashboard lets you manage details about your venue such as employee details, capacity and so on. You will also have access to a list of users who checked-in to your venue.

COVID-19

No, the app cannot tell you if you are suffering from COVID-19 or not. Even in the case of the slightest symptoms, we highly recommend you to get tested for COVID-19. The app is useful for tracking your outings and lets you know if you've visited a COVID-19 hotspot. In the event that you visit a hotspot, you will be contacted by a health official with further instructions. Please isolate in your home in the meantime.

The common symptoms of COVID-19 include fever, dry cough and tiredness. Some patients might also experience bodyaches, sore throat, diarrhoea, conjunctivitis, headache, loss of taste and smell, or a rash. It can take up to 2 weeks for symptoms to appear in an infected person.

Follow the national social distancing rules in your country at all times. Be mindful of restrictions, if any. In case of further queries when you're out, contact the venue manager at the establishment for clarification regarding venue capacity and their own COVID-19 guidelines, if any. Make sure you check-in to any venues you visit using a contact-tracing app.

Didn't find what you were looking for? Send us an email at covidapp@contacttracers.com and we'll get back to you in 3-4 business days.
