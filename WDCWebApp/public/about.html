Contact Tracers Australia

Pioneering safety

Contact Tracers was established with one goal in mind: to provide reliable, accurate contact-tracing services at zero cost to the citizen. Our designers worked tirelessly to come up with a UI that is as neat as it is easy to use. Say goodbye to apps that make you call your grandkids for help. No matter who you are - an individual citizen, a venue owner or a health official - Contact Tracers is THE ultimate App for you.

In these times of uncertainty, we believe in giving power to the citizen. Our contact-tracing app is designed purely with love (and code).

No loopholes to exploit your privacy. No shady location tracking. All our collected data goes straight to the government. We don't take a single peek. Promise.
